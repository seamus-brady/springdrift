## Code of Conduct

This project is open to everyone regardless of background, experience level, or identity.
Contributions are genuinely appreciated. Be respectful, constructive, and assume good
faith in others.

The maintainer has final say on all decisions regarding the project's direction,
accepted contributions, and community standards. Disagreements are welcome as
discussion, but decisions are not made by committee.

If this doesn't work for you, you are of course free to fork the project under
the terms of the AGPLv3.
